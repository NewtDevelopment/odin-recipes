Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

These are the best chocolate chip cookies that you will ever have!.

Back in the 1800's my great grandma used to fight off lions while making this recipe. It has been passed down from generation to generation. Please enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup softened butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup Brown Sugar
  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tsp hot water
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3 cups all purpose flour
  • 2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Cream together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla. Dissolve baking soda in hot water. Add to batter along with salt. Stir in flour, chocolate chips, and nuts. Drop by large spoonfuls onto ungreased pans.
  3. Bake for 10 minutes in the preheated oven, or until edges are nicely browned.
