Vanilla Ice Cream

Description

This is great ice cream that is fun to make

Get your entire family involved, and enjoy this summer treat together.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups half-andhalf or light cream
  • 1(14 ounce) can EAGLE BRAND Sweetened Condensed Milk
  • 2 tbls Vanilla Extract

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine ingredients; mix well. Pour into ice cream freezer container. Freeze according to manufacturer's instructions. Freeze leftovers.
