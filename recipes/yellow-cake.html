Yellow Cake

Description

Super moist, and fun to eat!

Have you even ate a cake that melts in your mouth? Now you can!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter
  • 2 1/2 cups white sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 1/2 cups buttermilk
  • 3 3/4 cups all purpose flour
  • 2 1/3 tsp baking powder
  • 2 1/2 tsp baking soda

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350. Grease and flour 2 9X13 inch pans. Sift together the flour, baking powder and baking soda. Set aside
  2. In a large bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla. Beat in the flour mixture alternately with the buttermilk, micing until incorporated. Pour the batter into prepared pans.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven for 35 to 40 minutes, or until a tothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clesn. Allow to cool.
